Design Principles

Rebecca Kilger

Visual Hierarchy

Zillow

zillow.com

Zillow does a good job of using visual hierarchy to emphasize their more commonly used features. One of their most used features is the search bar, so they have designed their page to have the large search bar as one of the first things you see when you visit their website.

White Space

Crumbl Cookies

crumblcookies.com

Crumble Cookies has a simple website that does a great job using white space to give it's site a clean look. They don't overwhelm you with too much color or clutter on their page, allowing you to focus on their products.

Simply Chocolate

simplychocolate.dk

Simply Chocolate uses its minimalist design to eliminate choices, making it more likely that those visiting their site will be able to quickly decide on a product. As you scroll through their site, you are always presented with just one product, meaning only one decision at a time.
