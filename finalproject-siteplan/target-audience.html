Site Purpose

The main purpose of this site is to provide users with information about the members of the Cedar Hills Chamber of Commerce and encourage other business owners within the community to become members themselves. Within this website, I will detail the benefits of joining the chamber and the different levels of membership, provide information promoting the city of Cedar Hills, and advertise businesses within the community. My website will also provide other local information, such as the weather and upcoming events.

Target Audience

The target audience for this site will consist of anyone looking for information regarding the weather and/or upcoming events within the areas of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. This audience has a wide range in age and circumstance. Some users will be looking for fun activities while visiting the area, while others may live in the area and will visit the site regularly to keep track of the weather. Because users will be visiting our website under various circumstances, it will need to be able to accomodate those using both computers and mobile devices.
