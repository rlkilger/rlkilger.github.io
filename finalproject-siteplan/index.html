Site Purpose

The main purpose of this site is to provide users with information about the members of the Cedar Hills Chamber of Commerce and encourage other business owners within the community to become members themselves. Within this website, I will detail the benefits of joining the chamber and the different levels of membership, provide a way of joining the chamber, give information promoting the city of Cedar Hills, and advertise businesses within the community. My website will also provide other local information, such as the weather and upcoming events.

Target Audience

The target audience for this site will include current members of the Cedar Hills Chamber of Commerce, those ready to join the chamber, and anyone who is looking for more information regarding the chamber and its members. This audience will mostly be business owners within the Cedar Hills community, and the age range will be pretty much anyone old enough to have started their own business. The types of businesses will vary greatly, meaning some of the business owners visiting my website might have minimal computer experience. Therefore, it would be best to keep the website simple and easy to navigate.
