Soda Springs Fire Department Foam in the Park

Come join us this Saturday at City Park for the Soda Springs Fire Department Foam in the Park! The fire department has volunteered to bring their working fire trucks to the park for this fun event. They will be allowing your little ones to explore the trucks and teaching them about fire safety. This will be a great learning experience for adults and children alike. We will gather around the south-east corner of the park for a demonstration where we will get to see firemen put out a real fire! There will also be a face painting station and other fun and games, including a water slip and slide. Don't forget your swimsuits! Bring your wallets to support your favorite local food trucks. Enjoy some of the city's amazing waffles, tacos, burgers, and ice cream.
