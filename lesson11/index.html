EIPH will no longer issue mask mandates

At a recent Eastern Idaho Public Health board meeting, the board decided that it will no longer be issuing county-wide mask mandates, but is instead asking the public to take personal responsibility and use their judgement to determine in what circumstances masks will be needed. With vaccines becoming more and more available and conditions improving signicantly since last year, the public health board beleives that it is no longer justified in enforcing mask restrictions on its citizens. Businesses and individuals are still asked to do their part in slowing the spread of COVID-19, including frequent hand washing, wearing masks when in densly-occupied areas, and staying home and getting tested when you are feeling ill or when you have had direct exposure to someone who has been tested positively with COVID-19.
