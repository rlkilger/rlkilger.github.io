That Famous Preston Night Rodeo

We are excited to announce the dates for the 2021 That Famous Preston Night Rodeo. The rodeo will begin on July 29th and will go for three nights. After having to cancel the rodeo last year due to the pandemic, many were concerned that COVID restrictions would make it impossible for many of the events to resume this year, but the EIPH has lifted many of the restrictions and has given the go-ahead for the planning of our favorite Preston night rodeo. Tickets will go on sale on May 31st. We're expecting a huge turnout, so don't miss out! The Preston Rodeo Committee is looking for volunteers to help with the planning of the rodeo. The rodeo uses volunteers for much of the rodeo, from manning the ticket station to participating in the Rodeo Days Parade.
